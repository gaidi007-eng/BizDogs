Who We Are

Business Students,
Built Different.

Bizdogs is the premier Living Learning Community for business-minded students at the University of Minnesota Duluth. We live together, learn together, and grow together — building a tight-knit network of future leaders right in the residence halls.

More than a club, we're a community. From networking events and guest speakers to study sessions and social events, Bizdogs gives you every advantage as you begin your business journey at UMD.

Community

Live alongside peers who share your goals and ambition.

Leadership

Develop real skills through hands-on opportunities.

Network

Connect with alumni, mentors, and industry professionals.

Success

Academic support systems built into your daily life.
