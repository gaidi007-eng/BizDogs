Why Join BizDogs?

  • To succeed! Research shows students who participate in learning communities have higher GPAs.

  • BizDogs students have a higher retention rate than other LSBE students.

  • Move into your residence hall before everyone else.

  • Interact with LSBE faculty and staff who want to help you successfully navigate your first year in college.

  • Participate in sessions and events, open only to BizDogs members, to advance your educational development and establish professional goals.

  • Network with LSBE alumni to gain insight on career paths and success strategies.

  • Attend study groups right on your floor within your BizDogs Community.

  • Meet a diverse group of students from all over the state, country and world who bring different backgrounds, thoughts, and ideas.

  • Develop lasting friendships with students you will be taking classes with.

  • Live with other students with similar academic interests.

  • Guaranteed seats in required BizDogs classes.
